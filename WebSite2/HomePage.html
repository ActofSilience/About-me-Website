Welcome to my site

This site serves as a little window into my interests and my life. Currently I work retail at a shop that sells car parts. I have always been mechanically inclined, so I also work on cars for friends and family. If its any indicator of my ability, last year I replaced the engine in my Audi after the water pump seized and shredded the timing belt. It was a lot of work, and took a month to complete because parts had to be rebuilt and reused, but I got it done and it is one of my proudest moments.
